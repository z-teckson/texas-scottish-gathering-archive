# 2023 Scottish Gathering of the Clans in Texas

**Date:** October 21–22, 2023  
**Location:** Heritage Park, Fredericksburg, Texas  
**Theme:** "Honoring the Jacobite Legacy"

## Overview

The 2023 gathering brought together over 2,000 participants from across Texas and neighboring states. The weekend celebrated the rich heritage of Scottish clans with traditional music, dance, athletic competitions, and historical re‑enactments.

## Participating Clans

- Clan Campbell
- Clan MacGregor
- Clan MacDonald
- Clan MacLeod
- Clan Wallace
- Clan Sinclair
- Clan MacKenzie
- Clan Cameron

## Highlights

### Opening Ceremony
The gathering opened with a parade of tartans, led by the Lone Star Pipe Band. The Texas State Senator gave a welcoming address highlighting the contributions of Scottish‑Americans to Texas history.

### Highland Games
Traditional heavy events including caber toss, stone put, and hammer throw were held on Saturday. The overall champion was **Ewan MacRae** (Clan MacRae).

### Ceilidh
A lively ceilidh on Saturday evening featured the band *“The Tartan Terrors”* and included Scottish country dancing, storytelling, and a whisky tasting hosted by the Texas Scotch Society.

### Clan Tents & Genealogy
Each participating clan maintained a tent where visitors could learn about their history, tartans, and family connections. The Scottish Heritage Society of Texas provided free genealogy research assistance.

## Special Guests
